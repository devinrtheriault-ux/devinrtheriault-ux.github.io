Satisfactory is a first-person-open-world factory building game with a dash of exploration and combat. Play alone or with friends, explore an alien planet, create multi-story factories, and enter conveyer belt heaven! Made by the developing team at Coffee Stain Studios.

Activities

  1. Spend hours building complicated factories.
  2. Embrace the spaghetti of conveyer belts.
  3. Rebuild, Rebuild, Rebuild

Available on PC,Playstion 5, and Xbox Series X/S.
